URC 2024

Team Vicharaka is a team by first and second year students in the IISc UG programmes to compete in the University Rover Challenge in 2024. The URC is the world's premier robotics competition for college students, held annually in the Mars Desert Research Station, Utah. URC challenges student teams to design and build the next generation of Mars rovers to work alongside astronauts on the Red Planet.
